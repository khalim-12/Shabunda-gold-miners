About Us

Shabunda Gold Miners also known as Shabunda Community Gold Miners is a village association of local gold miners in the Shabunda Community. Guided by the Head Chief of the Shabunda, the association operates with integrity, transparency, and a deep commitment to responsible mining practices. We are dedicated to promoting ethical gold mining and fair trade by ensuring that all our members and partners are recognized and endorsed by the local community. Our mission is to protect genuine miners and gold dealers from fraudulent activities and unethical practices by collaborating exclusively with trusted, certified gold refineries and smelters across the region. At Shabunda Community Gold Miners, we take pride in fostering trust, sustainability, and community empowerment ensuring that the wealth of our land benefits our people and future generations

  • Over 15 years of mining experience
  • 500+ community members employed
  • Environmentally responsible practices
  • Fair trade certified operations
